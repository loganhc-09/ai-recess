Questions

Frequently asked.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. Lovable is an AI builder. You type what you want in plain English, and it builds it. We'll be there to help you prompt it well.

What if I already have a website I hate?

Perfect. Bring it. You can rebuild from scratch in 90 minutes using what you've already got as a starting point.

What if I can't make it live?

You'll get the full recording and resource guide. But honestly, the magic is in doing it live with everyone else. That's the whole point. Don't bookmark this one.

Can I use a different tool besides Lovable?

We're standardizing on Lovable so we can support everyone effectively. If you're already set up with Claude Code or another tool, you're welcome to use it, but our guided instructions will be Lovable-specific.

What about hosting? Will my site cost money after this?

Lovable has a free tier that includes hosting. Your site will be live at a Lovable URL for free. Connecting your custom domain is a paid Lovable feature ($20/mo) or you can migrate to free hosting later. We'll cover options in the resource guide.

Will my site show up in AI search results?

We'll walk you through the basics during the session: meta tags, structured data, letting AI crawlers access your site. You'll also get our AEO checklist as a bonus. For the full setup (server-rendered pages, custom schema, the works), that's what Personal Website 2.0 is for, and Blitz attendees get a discount.

Is this just for people looking for jobs?

No. Founders, freelancers, creators, side-hustlers, career-changers. Anyone who wants to own their online presence instead of renting it from LinkedIn. Your personal website is your home base regardless of what you do.
